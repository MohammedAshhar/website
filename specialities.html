Our Laboratory Services

State-of-the-art diagnostic services with accurate and timely results

Clinical Biochemistry

Comprehensive blood chemistry analysis including kidney function, liver function, lipid profile, and blood sugar testing for accurate metabolic assessment.

Haematology

Complete blood count, coagulation studies, and specialized haematological investigations to evaluate overall health and detect blood disorders.

Immunology

Advanced immunological testing including C-reactive protein, thyroid function, and hormone assays for diagnosing infections and autoimmune conditions.

Microbiology

Culture sensitivity tests, bacterial identification, and antibiotic susceptibility testing to guide targeted treatment decisions.

Pathology

Histopathology and cytology services for tissue biopsy analysis, Pap smears, and comprehensive anatomical pathology reporting.

Radiology & Imaging

Digital X-ray, ultrasound, and other imaging modalities to support accurate diagnosis across all medical specialities.
